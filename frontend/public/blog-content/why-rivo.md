# Why We Chose the Name Rivo

Names do a lot of work. They shape first impressions before a visitor reads a single line of product copy.

When we started exploring alternatives, we wanted something shorter, cleaner, and easier to say out loud than a descriptive platform name.

## What we wanted

- A name that feels modern without sounding cold
- Something simple enough to remember after hearing it once
- A brand that could sit comfortably beside creative work instead of overpowering it

## Why Rivo fit

Rivo feels compact, clear, and brandable. It gives the product more room to grow while keeping the tone calm and design-led.

It also works better across a homepage, a logo, and a shared link. That matters when the product is built around presentation.

## What changes for users

The core product direction stays the same: quick setup, clean layouts, and a portfolio that keeps the focus on the work itself.

The new name simply gives the product a stronger identity around that goal.
