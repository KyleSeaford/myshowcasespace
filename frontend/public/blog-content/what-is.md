# What Is Rivo?

Rivo is a portfolio website platform designed for photographers, artists, students, and other creatives who want a clean place to present their work.

Instead of building a site from scratch, you can publish quickly with a layout that keeps the focus on your images and projects.

## What Rivo helps you do

- Create a professional portfolio in minutes
- Publish on a GetRivo.net address or your own custom domain
- Organize projects and update your work anytime
- Share a portfolio link that looks polished on desktop and mobile

## Who it is for

Rivo is built for people who care about visual presentation but do not want a complex website setup. It works well for personal portfolios, student showcases, and creative studios.

## Why it exists

Most website tools are either too technical or too cluttered. Rivo is intentionally minimal: calm layouts, clear structure, and fast publishing so your work is the hero.
